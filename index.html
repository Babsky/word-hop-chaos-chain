Word Hop: Chaos Chain

📖 How to Play
  • The Start: Begin with a word matching the assigned letter and length.
  • The Chain: Every subsequent word must begin with the last letter of the word you just entered.
  • The Chaos: The required word length shifts randomly each turn (anywhere from 4 to 10 letters)!
  • Sudden Death: Typing an invalid word, a misspelled word, a word of the wrong length, or a repeated word ends the game instantly!
  • The Goal: Successfully survive all 7 randomised length stages to win.
